## 1. KARBON

**Pricing**: $59-$89/user/month (annual) | $79-$99/user/month (monthly)
- Team Plan: $59/user/month (annual), $79/user/month (monthly)
- Business Plan: $89/user/month (annual), $99/user/month (monthly)
- 5-user firm estimate: $3,540-$5,340/year (annual billing)
- 15-user firm: $10,620-$16,020/year
- Snowflake data warehouse access on Business plan
- Multi-year discounts available

### 1.1 Client Portal Features

**Karbon for Clients** (launched July 2025 - now GA globally):

- **Secure Messaging**: In-context responses and updates on projects; clients communicate directly with firm about specific work items. Notifications via email, push, or in-app.
- **File Sharing**: Secure upload/download of documents through organized workflows. End-to-end enterprise-grade encryption.
- **E-Signatures**: Request electronic signatures from any device via secure link. Documents prepared and sent from within Karbon. Proposals/engagement letters sent through Karbon Engagements do NOT use e-signature credits.
- **Intake Forms / Questionnaires**: Integrated client organizers via StanfordTax integration embedded into workflows. Tax organizers rolling out H2 2025.
- **Task Lists for Clients**: Personalized task lists and timelines visible in portal. Clients see their to-do items with deadlines.
- **Mobile App**: Karbon for Clients app available on iOS (App Store). Push notifications supported.
- **Security**: Magic link authentication, optional 2FA, device-specific access controls. Enterprise-grade encryption.
- **Billing Dashboard**: Clients can view invoices, payment history, and billing status in portal.

### 1.2 Workflow / Automation

- **Task Templates**: Standardized process templates from Karbon Template Library. Customizable to match firm-specific workflows. Covers onboarding, tax prep, bookkeeping, advisory.
- **Recurring Workflows**: Automator feature moves tasks through predetermined workflow stages. Recurring work creation on schedule.
- **Automated Reminders**: AI-prioritized email notifications based on urgency and sentiment. Automatic client follow-ups.
- **Deadline Tracking**: Built into work items with due dates, budget tracking, and timeline visibility.
- **Status Boards (Kanban)**: Firm-wide Kanban board in Work section. Work items move through customizable status columns. Unlimited filters can be applied. Saved views for quick access.
- **Approval Chains**: Automator triggers approvals and notifications based on workflow stage completion.
- **Automators**: Conditional automations that trigger actions based on work status changes, task completion, or client actions. Can create tasks, send emails, move work between stages.
- **Integrated Workflows**: Activity from integrated apps (tax software, etc.) can automatically trigger work creation and updates within Karbon.

### 1.3 Time & Billing

- **Time Tracking**: Built-in timer against client or job. Multiple entry methods: timers, manual entry, bulk time allocation. Auto-suggestions based on behavior for forgotten time entries. Smart notes summarize tasks. AI auto-detects client or job context.
- **WIP (Work in Progress)**: Real-time WIP reporting. Automatic tracking of unbilled time and work.
- **Invoicing**: Time-and-materials billing, fixed-fee billing, recurring billing. Automated invoice generation from completed work. Review and approval workflow before sending.
- **Payment Processing**: Clients pay via credit card or ACH directly through Karbon. Invoices paid on average 9 days earlier than manual payment methods.
- **Write-up/Write-down**: Supported through realization tracking - compare billed vs. budgeted amounts.
- **Realization Reporting**: Automatic reporting on time realization and fixed-fee realization rates.
- **Fixed-Fee Billing**: Full support with budget-based tracking.
- **Subscription/Recurring Billing**: Weekly, monthly, upfront, upon completion, or custom billing schedules.
- **Rate Plans**: Multiple rate tables with different effective dates and role-specific rates.
- **Receivables Dashboard**: Aged receivables view showing unpaid invoices to identify cash flow issues.
- **Timesheet Approvals**: Period locking to ensure billing accuracy.

### 1.4 Client Management / CRM

- **Contact Management**: Client contacts synced with Xero/QBO. Auto-creation of new client contacts.
- **Entity Tracking**: Client-level organization with service categorization.
- **Service History**: Full work history per client visible in client profile.
- **Notes**: Internal notes system (not visible to clients). Full formatting, attachments, and links.
- **Tags**: Organizational tags for filtering and reporting.
- **Custom Fields**: Available for client and work categorization.
- **Relationships**: Client-contact relationships mapped. Team member assignments per client.
- **Client Brief**: AI-generated summary of emails, notes, work, and billing data into a single client brief.

### 1.5 Communication

- **Email Integration**: Deep Gmail and Microsoft 365 (Outlook) integration. All emails flow into central Triage inbox.
- **Triage**: Centralized inbox where emails become actionable work items. AI prioritizes based on urgency and sentiment. Delegate email tasks to team members.
- **Internal Chat**: Notes system for internal team conversations. @mention colleagues (@name, @all, @team_name). Comments on notes with formatting, attachments, links. Notifications for mentioned team members.
- **Client Messaging**: In-context messaging within work items. Clients respond through portal or email.
- **Automated Emails**: Email templates for workflow stages. AI-generated email drafts aligned with brand voice.
- **Bulk Communications**: Not a primary feature (integrates with other tools via Zapier).
- **SMS**: Not natively supported.

### 1.6 Proposals & Engagement Letters

- **Karbon Engagements** (Early Access, rolling out 2025-2026):
  - Custom proposal builder with personalized intro, services & pricing, clear terms & conditions
  - Branded proposals and engagement letters
  - Collect payment details upfront
  - E-signature collection (no credit usage for Engagements)
  - Once signed, automatic work creation and task assignment
  - Configurable billing schedules (weekly, monthly, upfront, upon completion, custom)
- **GoProposal Integration**: Third-party integration for instant proposals and automatic engagement letters with flexible pricing.
- **Ignition Integration**: Alternative proposal and engagement letter platform with Karbon connector.

### 1.7 Reporting

- **Staff Utilization**: Capacity budget per team member. Billable vs. non-billable time split. Performance against capacity goals.
- **Capacity Planning**: Resource allocation to drive utilization over 70%. Visual capacity dashboard.
- **Revenue by Service**: Revenue tracking broken down by service type.
- **Client Profitability**: Profitability measurement by firm, staff, and service. Compare performance across client portfolios.
- **Team Performance**: Common metrics: work velocity, budget vs. actuals, work completion time, client response time.
- **Deadline Compliance**: Work completion tracking against deadlines.
- **Practice Intelligence**: Pre-built dashboards for realization, utilization, revenue, capacity. Customizable with filters and date ranges. Create dashboards from scratch.
- **Data Warehouse**: Snowflake connector for custom BI reporting (Power BI, Tableau, etc.).
- **KPI Dashboards**: Templates available in Karbon Template Library for custom KPI tracking.

### 1.8 Integrations

- **QuickBooks Online**: Client sync, billing/payment sync. Manual payments from QBOA sync to Karbon.
- **Xero**: Deep integration - client contacts auto-matched, invoices synced, payments auto-synced bidirectionally.
- **Tax Software**: Truss integration for tax workflow automation. StanfordTax for client organizers and workpaper management.
- **Calendars**: Not natively integrated (available through Zapier).
- **Zapier**: Full Zapier support connecting to thousands of apps.
- **API**: REST API with webhooks. Supported languages: Java, JavaScript, Objective-C, C#, PHP, Python, Ruby.
- **GoProposal / Ignition**: Proposal and engagement letter creation.
- **Other**: Slack, Microsoft Teams (via Zapier).

### 1.9 AI Features

- **Email Summarization**: AI-generated bite-sized summaries of long email threads and internal discussions.
- **Email Drafting**: AI-generated replies aligned with brand voice.
- **Smart Suggestions**: Task assignment suggestions based on team expertise and past work patterns. Auto-suggestions for time entries.
- **Client Briefs**: AI summarizes emails, notes, work, and billing into single client brief.
- **Priority Detection**: AI prioritizes emails based on urgency and sentiment analysis.
- **Workflow Intelligence**: Activity from integrated apps automatically triggers work creation.
- **Time Savings**: Average 18.5 hours saved per employee per week ($34,688/year per employee).

## 6. ESSENTIAL MODERN FEATURES FOR PREMIUM CPA FIRMS

Based on industry research for 2025-2026, these are the features premium CPA firms ($20K+/year spend) consider essential:

### 6.1 AI-Powered Transaction Categorization

- **Industry Standard**: 99%+ accuracy in document processing and transaction categorization.
- **Leaders**: Botkeeper (AI + human review), Docyt (end-to-end bookkeeping automation), Extend (AI expense categorization).
- **Impact**: 90% reduction in manual errors after AI implementation.
- **Current State**: QBO and Xero have basic categorization. Standalone tools (Botkeeper, Vic.ai) handle heavy lifting. Canopy's new Bookkeeping module (beta 2026) brings GL visibility into practice management.

### 6.2 Automated Bank Feed Reconciliation

- **Industry Standard**: Autonomous reconciliation agents that auto-match transactions, flag exceptions.
- **Leaders**: Botkeeper, Docyt, DualEntry (AI account reconciliation).
- **Impact**: Firms using integrated AI workflows report 40% faster cycle times.
- **Integration Point**: Must connect to bank feeds via Plaid/Yodlee and cross-reference with GL.

### 6.3 Client Self-Service Capabilities

- **Industry Standard**: Clients should be able to complete 80%+ of routine interactions without firm involvement.
- **Must-Have Features**:
  - Document upload (drag-and-drop, mobile camera)
  - E-signature completion
  - Invoice payment (one-click)
  - Questionnaire/organizer completion
  - Appointment booking
  - Secure messaging
  - Progress tracking (where is my return?)
  - Document download (tax returns, reports)
- **Best-in-Class**: TaxDome (document paywall, appointment booking, full self-service). Canopy Smart Intake (AI-prefilled forms).

### 6.4 Automated Late Payment Reminders

- **Industry Standard**: Multi-channel reminder sequences (email + SMS + portal notification).
- **Must-Have Features**:
  - Configurable reminder schedule (before due, on due, after due)
  - Escalating reminder sequences
  - Grace period configuration
  - Late fee auto-calculation
  - SMS reminders (not just email)
  - Dashboard for overdue amounts
- **Best-in-Class**: TaxDome (email + SMS + portal + document paywall). Canopy (automated invoice reminders with scheduling).

### 6.5 Revenue Recognition

- **Industry Standard**: Automated revenue recognition per ASC 606.
- **Must-Have Features**:
  - Deferred revenue tracking for prepaid engagements
  - Revenue recognition schedules (monthly, upon delivery)
  - WIP to revenue conversion
  - Retainer management
  - Revenue recognition reporting
- **Current Gap**: None of the four platforms handle full ASC 606 revenue recognition. Most CPA firms use QBO/Xero for actual accounting and practice management tools for billing/invoicing.

### 6.6 Multi-Currency Support

- **Industry Standard**: For firms with international clients.
- **Must-Have Features**:
  - Multiple currency invoicing
  - Exchange rate management (auto-update)
  - Foreign currency GL entries
  - Multi-currency reporting
- **Current Gap**: Not a primary feature in any of the four platforms (they assume USD/domestic). Handled at the accounting software layer (QBO/Xero).

### 6.7 Budgeting and Forecasting

- **Industry Standard**: Advisory-tier feature for premium firms.
- **Leaders**:
  - Jirav: All-in-one forecasting, budgeting, reporting, dashboarding. Purpose-built for accounting/CFO advisory.
  - Fathom: Reporting, KPIs, forecasting, consolidation. Multi-currency support.
  - Spotlight Reporting: Reporting, KPIs, forecasting, consolidation.
- **Must-Have Features**:
  - Rolling forecasts and scenario planning
  - Budget vs. actual variance analysis
  - Cash flow forecasting
  - Industry benchmarking
  - Client-facing dashboard exports
- **Integration**: These are typically add-on tools that connect to QBO/Xero, not built into practice management platforms.

### 6.8 Advisory / KPI Dashboards for Clients

- **Industry Standard**: Moving from compliance-only to advisory-first model.
- **Must-Have Features**:
  - Client-facing KPI dashboards (revenue, margins, cash flow, AR/AP aging)
  - Predictive cash flow models
  - Customized tax savings recommendations
  - Sector-specific benchmarking
  - Automated alerts for anomalies
  - Monthly reporting packages (auto-generated)
- **Leaders**: Jirav, Fathom, Spotlight Reporting, Karbon KPI Dashboard Templates.
- **Trend**: Advisory intelligence platforms that tell practitioners "what to do next" for every client, with prioritized alerts. Firms seeing entire book of business monitored in one place.

### 6.9 Agentic AI / Autonomous Workflows (Emerging 2025-2026)

- **Industry Trend**: Agentic AI systems that manage entire accounting workflows autonomously.
- **Capabilities**:
  - Auto-reconcile accounts
  - Draft audit reports
  - Track compliance timelines
  - Anomaly detection and alerting
  - Predictive analytics
- **Adoption**: Early stage. CPA.com 2025 AI Report shows growing adoption but mostly for document processing and categorization. Full autonomous workflows are 1-2 years away from mainstream.

- Transaction approval workflow

- Check printing

- Automated backup system
- Role-based access (CPA_OWNER, ASSOCIATE)
- React frontend with all workflow pages

### Features the GA CPA System Is MISSING (compared to premium platforms):

#### HIGH PRIORITY (Core Practice Management):
1. **Client Portal** - No client-facing portal. Clients cannot:
   - Upload documents themselves
   - View/sign documents electronically
   - See their task lists or progress
   - Pay invoices online
   - Send secure messages
   - Access reports or tax returns

4. **Time Tracking & Billing** - No time tracking. No WIP reporting. No invoicing to clients. No payment processing. No realization or utilization reporting.

5. **Email Integration** - No Gmail/Outlook sync. No triage/centralized inbox. No email-to-task conversion.

6. **Proposals & Engagement Letters** - No proposal builder. No engagement letter templates. No automated onboarding from signed proposals.

7. **Secure Client Messaging** - No in-app messaging. No SMS. No client communication channel beyond email.

8. **Client Request System** - No automated information/document request workflow. No questionnaires/organizers.

#### MEDIUM PRIORITY (Operational Excellence):
9. **Capacity Planning** - No staff utilization tracking. No workload balancing. No capacity dashboards.

10. **Staff Performance Reporting** - No utilization reports. No realization tracking. No revenue-per-service analysis.

11. **Recurring Task/Workflow Management** - No recurring job scheduling (monthly bookkeeping, quarterly filings).

12. **Invoice Reminders** - No automated payment reminder sequences.

13. **Client Profitability Analysis** - No per-client profitability tracking. No service-level revenue analysis.

14. **Kanban/Pipeline Views** - No visual workflow board for tracking job status across firm.

#### LOWER PRIORITY (Competitive Differentiation):
15. **AI Document Recognition** - No automatic document categorization/renaming.

16. **Advisory/KPI Dashboards** - No client-facing analytics or benchmarking.

17. **Budgeting & Forecasting** - No budget vs. actual analysis for clients.

18. **Multi-Currency** - Not needed for Georgia-focused firm.

19. **Automated Bank Feeds** - No direct bank connection for auto-reconciliation.

20. **Mobile App** - No native mobile experience for firm or clients.
